Hi! My name is Samuel Quesada, a huge enthusiast of video games since I was a little kid. Since I enter university, and discover the world of video game programming, I have made some personal projects. I have found, with a group of colleagues, the indie studio Malaka Studio. Together, we took part in some events like CPCRetrodev 2019, GMTK Jam 2020 and SGD Jam II. Thanks to these events, we had some incredible experiences, developed various games and gain some experience in this wonderful industry.



I have studied in the Univerity of Alicante, Spain, and I have in my own a bachelor's degree in with a specialty in video game development. Apart from the video games development, I have also knowledge in general programming, having experience with Java, C, C++, C#, HTML, CSS and JavaScript languages and also some familiarity with PHP and assembly. Proving some of the knowledge, I have made this website by my own (yeah, I know, it's not the best thing in the world, but it's something). In addition, I have worked in a software development project relating with security cameras in Peka Sistemas Informaticos A Medida. Lastly, I have a Cambridge Advanced certificate in English (C1), so I can defend myself without much trouble in any situation.



Most of the work I have done in the video game development is as gameplay programmer and AI programmer. I have mostly created all the mechanics in the projects I have been involved, and made a substantial part of the AI behaviour, but I have also made some 3D models and some pixel art in some projects, although I'm not an artist myself and I have little to no idea on this subject, to be honest. As I said in the Home page, I'm currently looking for improving my experiencie in the video game industry, as a junior gameplay programmer, so, if you like my profile and the projects I have done, feel free to contact me.
